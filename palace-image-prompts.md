## Tips for Best Results

### Midjourney

```
--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw --s 250
```

### DALL-E 3
Use exactly as written. May need to generate multiple times for best result.

### Leonardo AI
Use "Photorealistic" model, guidance scale 7-8.

### General Tips
1. **Consistency:** Use similar lighting descriptions across all prompts ("warm golden lighting")
2. **Style:** Keep "photorealistic, shallow depth of field" in all prompts
3. **Aspect Ratio:** All should be 16:9 landscape
4. **Color Grading:** Warm, golden, inviting tones throughout
